“What kind of computer do I need to be an online SNHU student?”

A big advantage to being an SNHU online student is that there are no set class times, so you’re free to study around your weekly schedule — and work wherever you have access to a computer and the internet.

You’ll need to make sure you have access to a computer and the internet before the start of your first term. Specifically, here is what you’ll need to have for most degree programs:

Laptop or Desktop

Windows or Apple Operating system. 8GB RAM and 100GB storage

Internet Access

About 15 hours per week per course (recommended). 5 MBPS download speed.

Anti-Virus Software

Your internet service provider may include this with your subscription.

Note:  Chromebooks (Chrome OS) and iPads (iOS) do not meet the minimum requirements for coursework at SNHU. These offer limited functionality and do not work with some course technologies. They are not acceptable as the only device you use for coursework. While these devices are convenient and may be used for some course functions, they cannot be your primary device.


Pro Tip: Students who use the computers that mean minimum recommended requirements have a higher chance of success in their courses. (Based on May 2024 success study.)

Some degree programs have additional requirements

Some programs, like business analytics, game design, graphic design and computer science, require specialty software, a more powerful computer and other equipment (like a camera). Please see specific requirements for your program below.
